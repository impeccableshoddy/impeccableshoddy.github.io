How This Grew

On the making of this space

Like all great things come to be, this site is a direct result of my then due impending university assignment. I have always sort of wanted to write for some time now. Never knew the how, specially when I didn't have the what. But somehow with the magical pressure of deadline, here we are. And while this started as a reaction, I hope to end this as a choice. Going forward I shall work to whack at these bafflegabs and try to derive some god forbid sense from them. Because while different, I think it's moderately safe to assume we are all plagued by these flapdoodles in our quotidian existence. And while it's completely fair and fine to be prim and proper and let them be, I'm sort of what they call a rabble-rouser ya see? not to mention it tickles me the wrong way to have something so erratic dictate the course of my life. And I'm also painfully aware of this privilege which many do not get to have, to have time as abundant as to question the finer and more optional things in life. Thus through this space I hope to best utilize this privilege, and keep accounts of such occurrences and what I make of them.
